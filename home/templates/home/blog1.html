5 Winter-to-Spring Fashion Trends to Try Now

By Admin 3 Comments

As the seasons shift from frosty mornings to blossoming afternoons, your wardrobe deserves a seamless transition. Our Vogue Nest stylists reveal five key trends to bridge winter and spring with effortless sophistication.

1. Layered Lightweight Knits

Swap bulky sweaters for fine-gauge knits in pastel hues. Try a lilac cashmere turtleneck under a cropped blazer for a chic, modern look.

Pro Tip:

Layer a sleeveless knit vest over floral midi dresses for unexpected texture play.

2. Transitional Trench Coats

The timeless trench gets a 2024 update with bold silhouettes and fresh textures:

  • Cropped lengths in honeyed leather
  • Translucent PVC overlays for a futuristic touch
  • Belted waistlines with oversized buckles
Try This:

Pair a cropped trench with high-waisted tailored trousers and loafers for a polished city look.
